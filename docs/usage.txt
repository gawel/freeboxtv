Usage
========

Get channels list::

  $ fbxtv -l

Launch the full playlist::

  $ fbxtv

Launch a channel::

  $ fbxtv <channel_id|channel_name>

For example::  

  $ fbxtv nova # launch radio nova

  $ fbxtv france 2 # launch France 2

Close vlc::

  $ fbxtv -s

Launch freeplayer server::

  $ fbxtv -p
